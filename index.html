Intro

Hi there! My name is Carson Trinh. I was born and raised in San Jose, CA. Currently, I'm attending UC Berkeley, where I'm finishing up my bachelor's degree in Computer Science.

After graduating, I will be moving to the opposite side of the country to New York City, where I'll be joining Bloomberg L.P. as a full-time software engineer. It's a huge change, and I'm incredibly excited to start this new chapter of my life.

I like to do all sorts of things in my free time, although I'm currently focusing on just a handful of hobbies. The flavor of the year is photography, which I've taken more seriously for the past few months (fun fact: all of the pictures on this website are mine! Except for the ones of me, of course). I'm also a huge fan of Halo, the MCU, and Harry Potter!

Work

Where am I going?

I'll be joining Bloomberg L.P. in Midtown Manhattan as a full-time software engineer this August. I don't yet know what I'll be doing as the team selection process doesn't happen until I begin, though I do have a preference for application-based projects. We'll see!

Why Bloomberg?

Choosing what my first job out of school would be has been one of the most important decisions of my life thus far, so naturally I had to think very hard about what I wanted for my future. Working as an engineer at Bloomberg appeals to me in many aspects, at least when it comes to the impressions I got while visiting the office and speaking to the engineers there. I feel like I will have a lot of room to grow here, both professionally at Bloomberg and personally as an young adult living in New York City on my own. My goal over the next many years is to develop myself as a person, and that was the understanding I had in mind when accepting the offer.
